Platform Features

AI Cost Estimation

Upload photos of your space and receive an instant, AI-powered itemized cost breakdown — kitchen, bath, full remodel, and more. Know what you're getting into before the first call.

AI Chatbot Advisor

Describe your project in plain language. Our AI chatbot understands your needs and recommends specific, vetted local contractors by name — not a generic list of strangers.

Smart Contractor Discovery

Search and filter by specialty, location, rating, and verification status. Sponsored contractors are clearly labeled and shown separately — organic rankings are always earned, never paid.

Pay your deposit securely through the app via Stripe. Funds go straight to your contractor's account, with full refund support if plans change. Our transparent 8% platform fee is charged on top, so contractors keep their full deposit.

Chat directly with businesses before committing. Ask questions, share photos, and get a feel for the contractor — all within the app, with your number never shared.

Verified Reviews & Ratings

Real ratings from real homeowners. Contractors are sorted by verification status and average rating — not by who paid the most. The best contractors rise to the top on merit.
